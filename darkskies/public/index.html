Best nights to see the galaxy

A nightly score from September to November 2026 based on how dark the moon is, the historical odds of clear West Texas skies, and whether the bright galactic core is still above the horizon.

Prime galaxy nights Good nights Bright-moon / low-core nights New moon
Recommended window: Sept 9 – 14, 2026. The Sept 10 new moon lands just as summer monsoon clouds taper off, while the galactic core still stands tall in the evening sky — the sweet spot. By the Oct 10 new moon the skies are clearest of all, but the core sits low and sets early. November brings gorgeous dark, clear nights, yet the Milky Way's bright center is essentially gone until spring — lovely for planets and constellations, just past prime for the galaxy itself.

Same night, different elevations

September averages — every 1,000 ft up trades heat for cool
Big Bend is "three parks in one": the desert floor runs about 20°F hotter by day than the Chisos Basin. Nights are more even — and by November the basin is actually warmer after dark than the low desert, where cold air pools. Bars span each zone's typical overnight low → daytime high.

On the ground

Getting around & where to stay

Fly into Midland (MAF), grab a vehicle, and it's roughly a 4–5 hour drive south. A great first move: everyone converges on a gateway spot for night one, then we caravan into the park together.

Getting there

✈️

Midland Int'l Air & Space Port (MAF)

The closest airport — about 275 mi / 4½–5 hrs from the park.

Where to stay

🏨
Marathon · ~3 hr from MAF

The Gage Hotel

Historic 1927 adobe hotel, once named Texas's #1. Salt-water pool, 27-acre gardens, superb restaurant — the classic gateway, 42 mi from the park. Visit ↗
🏛️
Fort Davis · ~2½ hr from MAF

Indian Lodge

White-adobe lodge built by the CCC in the Davis Mountains, right by McDonald Observatory. Pool, trails, Black Bear Restaurant. Visit ↗
♨️
Near Presidio · off-grid

Chinati Hot Springs

Remote desert casitas around natural 90–100°F soaking pools. Dark skies, total quiet — an unforgettable soak after travel. Visit ↗
🌵
Terlingua · at the park's door

Terlingua Ghost Town

Stone-and-adobe casitas in a quirky old mining town at the west entrance — sunset porches and the best star-watching perches. Explore ↗
